GUNTUR

Guntur is a vibrant city located in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, historical significance, and its contribution to the agricultural landscape of India.

Activities to do at Guntur:

  1. Amaravati Buddhist Heritage
  2. Explore Undavalli Caves
  3. Kondavid Fort
  4. Mangalagiri Temple

Food to try in Guntur

  • Andhra Biryani
  • Andhra Fish Curry
  • Bobbatlu (Puran Poli)
  • Pulihora Dosa
